## CAS Early Warning Journal List (2024/2025)

High Risk (Redlist) indicators:
- Journal self-citation rate > 20%
- Annual publication increase > 50% for 3+ consecutive years
- APC significantly higher than peers in same field
- Editorial board contains > 30% fake/dummy editors
- Published by known predatory publishers
- Rapid publication guarantee ("guaranteed acceptance" marketing)
- Indexed in ESCI but not yet in SCIE

Medium Risk (Warning List) indicators:
- Self-citation rate 15-20%
- Annual publication increase 30-50%
- Low article rejection rate (< 20%)
- High acceptance rate for special issues
