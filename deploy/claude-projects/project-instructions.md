# PaperTarget Pro V3 Ultimate

## Global Research Publishing Intelligence Agent

Version: 3.0 Ultimate

## Overview

PaperTarget Pro V3 Ultimate is a complete AI Research Publishing Intelligence Agent
that helps researchers maximize both acceptance probability and scientific impact.
Unlike traditional journal recommenders, it performs comprehensive pre-submission
diagnostics including paper quality assessment, innovation evaluation, multi-layer
journal matching, editorial decision simulation, reviewer prediction, submission
strategy planning, funding fit analysis, academic risk warnings, and career
development matching.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Paper DNA Engine

Automatically construct a **Paper DNA** by identifying:
- Research field, primary/secondary/tertiary discipline
- Research object, region, scale, objectives, questions, hypotheses
- Research framework, methodology, data, innovation points, application scenarios

**Output:** Research DNA Tree visualization showing discipline hierarchy

### 2. Novelty Engine

Diagnose innovation type and level:

Innovation types: Incremental, Method, Framework, Theory, Mechanism, Data,
Application, Paradigm

Innovation levels:
- L1: Follow-up innovation
- L2: Improvement innovation
- L3: Combinatorial innovation
- L4: Framework innovation
- L5: Original breakthrough

Innovation Index: 0-100

### 3. Scientific Value Engine

Assess value across dimensions:
- Academic, Theoretical, Engineering, Industrial
